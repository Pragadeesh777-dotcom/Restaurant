Chicken curry is a flavorful dish featuring tender chicken pieces cooked in a spiced sauce made with onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili. Variations add creamy coconut milk, yogurt, tomatoes, or cream for extra richness. It pairs perfectly with rice or flatbread.




French fries are batonnet or julienne-cut potatoes, deep-fried to a fluffy inside and crispy outside. Traditionally prepared via a two-stage frying process—first at low heat to cook through, then at high heat for golden crispness. They’re served hot, salted, and accompanied by ketchup, mayonnaise, or vinegar as a side dish.


A classic Italian dish, pizza consists of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various toppings. Originating from Naples, it has become a global favorite with countless variations. Traditional Neapolitan pizza features a thin, soft crust and is typically cooked in a wood-fired oven, giving it a unique flavor and texture.
